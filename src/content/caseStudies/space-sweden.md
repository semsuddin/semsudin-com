## Overview

SPACE Sweden — a gaming rental platform with a strong themed product identity — needed automated testing established from the ground up, with a runway into mobile and performance validation.

## What I delivered

- **Foundational web automation suites**, built to be extended rather than rewritten as the product evolved
- **Mobile platform coverage** via Appium — Android and iOS parity for key user flows
- **Load testing integration** — worked alongside developers to identify performance bottlenecks before they reached production
- Agile collaboration across a small, fast-moving team

## Why it matters

The foundational work at SPACE Sweden is the kind that rarely makes it into case studies — because it's invisible when it's done right. Two years of suite design decisions, and the client kept growing on top of them without a rewrite. That's the win.
